---
title: Protocol Treasury
description: Treasury mandate, operating principles, and allocation governance.
---

The protocol treasury manages Yieldra-owned assets to support long-term protocol growth, operations, and resilience.

## Treasury Mandate

- Preserve runway for core development and security.
- Fund ecosystem growth, partnerships, and incentive programs.
- Maintain transparent reporting and auditable decision-making.

## Allocation Principles

- Prioritize sustainability over short-term spending.
- Keep reserve buffers for market and operational risk.
- Route major allocation changes through governance.

## Transparency Standards

- Treasury wallets are publicly disclosed.
- Material transfers are documented with context.
- Governance decisions define spending limits and update cadence.
