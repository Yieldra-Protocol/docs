#### Interest Rate Curves (target utilization, start %, target %, max %) <a href="#interest-rate-curves-target-utilization-start-target-max" id="interest-rate-curves-target-utilization-start-target-max"></a>

* Target utilization: the % where the interest starts increasing faster
* Start %: borrowing rate at 0% utilization
* Target %: borrowing rate at target utilization
* Max %: borrowing rate at 100% utilization
