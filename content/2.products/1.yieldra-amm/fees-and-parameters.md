## **BNB Chain (Coming Soon!)**

### **Fees**

* **Swap Fee**: 0.24%
* **Fee Distribution:**
  * 0.18% to liquidity providers
  * 0.06% to protocol

### **Multi-Utilization of Liquidity**

* **Liquidity redistribution:** the portion of liquidity in the ULP that is distributed towards lending
  * 30%
* **Update threshold**: the deviation from the target liquidity redistribution to trigger an update
  * 10%
