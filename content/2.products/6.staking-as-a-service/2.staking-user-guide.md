### How the Reward Pool Works

![Reward pool overview](https://images.yieldra.io/docs/staking-guide/yieldra-staking-1.webp)

* Your pending rewards are shown in the reward token, this is the amount available to be claimed
* The amount of tokens staked in total is also shown
* The amount of active staking power refers to the total staking power of all users, before rewards start this will show as "0"
* The Est. Max APR refers to the current APR for someone who has staked for the maximum amount of time, please note the APR will decrease proportionally as more staking power becomes active
* The remaining rewards show how much is left in the rewards pool for this staking pool

![Reward epoch details](https://images.yieldra.io/docs/staking-guide/yieldra-staking-2.webp)

* Each "epoch" a percentage of the reward pool is distributed to all stakers proportionally based on their "staking power", the amount of rewards per "epoch" is displayed in the Reward Per Epoch
* Each time a stake is created, there is a deposit fee
* Each pool might have a different epoch duration, please make sure to check before staking
* Each pool may be on a different chain, please make sure before staking
* The token address is displayed for your convenience

### 1) Choose how many tokens to stake

![Choose amount to stake](https://images.yieldra.io/docs/staking-guide/yieldra-staking-3.webp)

* This is your base staking power, the more you stake the higher your rewards will be
* Please note there is a small deposit fee for each pool set by the project

### 2) Choose how long to stake for

![Choose staking duration](https://images.yieldra.io/docs/staking-guide/yieldra-staking-4.webp)

* Your base staking power will be multiplied by a "time factor", based on how long you stake for, notice how a longer stake increases your Est. Power Gain, for full parameters check out our [List of Current Partners](https://docs.yieldra.io/products/staking-as-a-service/list-of-current-partners).
* The UI will display your staking power based on your staked amount and staked time
* Your staking power does not decrease over time
* Your tokens can be unstaked for free any time after your stake time is finished, but you will no longer earn rewards
* You may still unstake your tokens early for a penalty based on time remaining

### 3) Claiming rewards

![Claim rewards](https://images.yieldra.io/docs/staking-guide/yieldra-staking-5.webp)

* You may now claim your rewards every epoch, they do not disappear if you don't claim them each epoch but will not compound either
* Please note: you will not earn any rewards during the first epoch of your stake

### 4) Unstaking

![Unstaking flow](https://images.yieldra.io/docs/staking-guide/yieldra-staking-6.webp)

* You may unstake at any time for a penalty based on your time remaining, this is calculated as:
  base fee + (variable fee \* epochs remaining)
* If your stake has expired, you will continue to earn rewards indefinitely and you may also unstake at any time for free
