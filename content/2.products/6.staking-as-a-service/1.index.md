#### **For first time users, please visit the** [**user guide**](https://docs.yieldra.io/products/staking-as-a-service/staking-user-guide)

Yieldra Staking as a Service allows any community to become a **sponsor** and create a staking incentive for their token without any coding.

This may be a great tool for co-marketing initiatives such as staking Token A to get token B, and staking Token B to get Token A, or simply used within your community to create utility and reward long term believers of your project.

The Yieldra Staking as a Service uses the same staking incentive model as [Yieldra Staking](https://docs.yieldra.io/yieldra-token-yra/staking-yieldra-power), which utilizes a time-weighted staking model with a staking power measure to calculate rewards. However, all parameters are customizable.

## Customizability

Firstly, [here](https://docs.yieldra.io/yieldra-token-yra/staking-yieldra-power) is how staking works with the official Yieldra Token (no TGE yet)

* Duration of epochs: how long it takes for one reward period
* Max staking time: set the max staking time in terms of epochs
* Time factor: set how much bonus a user gets for each additional epoch staked
* Rewards per epoch: set the amount of rewards shared between users per epoch
* Early withdraw penalty: set base penalty + penalty per epoch remaining

An example of parameters can be found on the Yieldra Staking page, these parameters will be the the live parameters for the YRA token. However, communities can come up with entirely custom models such as a 5 minute epoch with maximum stake length of 8064 epochs (1 week), or even shorter duration staking pools.

## Marketing Opportunities for the Sponsor

* Be seen in the list of current ongoing projects
* Add links to your socials on the staking pools page
* More viewers on your project as more projects join the staking page
* Possibilities involving co-marketing and co-sponsors

## Monetization Opportunities for the Sponsor

* Deposit fee: 0.2%
  * 50% of all deposit fees go to the sponsor
* Early withdraw fee: custom base fee + variable fee based on time remaining
  * 75% of all early withdraw fees go to the sponsor

## Yieldra Protocol Fees

* Initial pool set up fee: **free for a limited time**
* Deposit fee: 0.2%
  * 50% of all deposit fees go to Yieldra
* Early withdraw fee: custom as described above
  * 25% of all early withdraw fees go to Yieldra
