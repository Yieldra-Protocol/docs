---
title: YRA Community Incentives
description: How community incentives are allocated and adjusted over time.
---

The YRA community incentive program is designed with flexible allocations that adapt to ecosystem needs and growth phases. While the current framework earmarks **10% for staking rewards**, **40% for airdrops**, **40% for yield farming**, and **10% for social/miscellaneous incentives**, these allocations are **not fixed in stone**.

Final allocations for each category will be **specified and updated within this section**, ensuring transparency as incentives evolve alongside protocol performance and community priorities.
