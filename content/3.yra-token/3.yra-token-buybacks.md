---
title: YRA Token Buybacks
description: Buyback framework, funding sources, and execution transparency principles.
---


YRA buybacks are designed to convert a portion of protocol value capture into sustained market support for the token.

## Objectives

- Reinforce long-term alignment between protocol usage and token value.
- Reduce circulating supply pressure when conditions allow.
- Keep execution transparent, predictable, and governance-auditable.

## Funding Sources

- Protocol revenue allocated by governance.
- Treasury-approved strategic allocations.
- Campaign-specific allocations approved by the community.

## Execution Principles

- Buybacks execute on-chain whenever possible.
- Wallets and transaction references are published for verification.
- Cadence and sizing follow treasury risk constraints, not discretionary market timing.

## Governance Controls

- The DAO can adjust cadence, budget caps, and distribution policy.
- Emergency pauses can be triggered under defined risk events.
- Parameter updates are announced before activation.
